E-commerce Optimization: A DMAIC Project

Improving the Digital Customer Journey for Arviso Ventures

D

Define: The Problem & Goal

Establishing the project's foundation by clearly articulating the problem and defining measurable goals.

Project Charter Summary

Business Case

The business has an inconsistent and difficult-to-measure Return on Investment (ROI) from its digital channels, limiting growth.

Problem Statement

A low e-commerce conversion rate (**1.66%**) and a low returning customer rate (**6.67%**) result in significant lost revenue and prevent the development of a loyal customer base.

Project Goal & Scope

Goal Statement (SMART)

To increase the conversion rate to 3% and the returning customer rate to 15%, aiming for an overall ROI increase of 50% by the end of the semester.

Project Scope

In-Scope: The end-to-end digital customer journey, including website analytics, SEO, and retention strategies.

Out-of-Scope: All manual fulfillment processes and in-person pop-up events.

Validated Root Causes

The funnel data validates that the most significant problems are:

  • A lack of initial trust on product pages.
  • High friction on the cart & checkout pages.
  • No post-purchase process to encourage repeat business.
