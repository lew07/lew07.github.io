E-commerce Optimization: A DMAIC Project

Improving the Digital Customer Journey for Arviso Ventures

D

Define: The Problem & Goal

Establishing the project's foundation by clearly articulating the problem and defining measurable goals.

Project Charter Summary

Business Case: The business has an inconsistent and difficult-to-measure Return on Investment (ROI) from its digital channels, limiting growth.

Problem Statement: A low e-commerce conversion rate (**1.66%**) and a low returning customer rate (**6.67%**) result in significant lost revenue and prevent the development of a loyal customer base.

Goal Statement (SMART): To increase the conversion rate to 3% and the returning customer rate to 15%, aiming for an overall ROI increase of 50% by the end of the semester.

Validated Root Causes

The data validates that the most significant problems are a lack of initial trust on product pages (causing high initial drop-off), high friction on the cart/checkout pages, and a lack of a post-purchase process to encourage repeat business.

I

Improve: Developing Solutions

Combining a foundational strategic change with tactical optimizations to maximize ROI.

Strategic Solution: Unify Brand Websites

Merge the Original Landlords site into RVSO78.com to consolidate SEO authority, improve brand discovery, and simplify operations. This is the foundational improvement.

Tactical Solutions (for the unified site)

Add Trust Signals & Reviews

Build social proof to increase visitor confidence.

Streamline Checkout

Enable guest checkout and simplify forms to reduce friction.

Implement Email/SMS Capture

Use a pop-up with an incentive to build a marketing list for repeat purchases.
