Project 1: Streamline Manual Order Fulfillment

This project targets the most time-consuming manual processes to reclaim valuable hours, reduce costs, and improve consistency for wholesale and trademark-sensitive orders.

Current Fulfillment Process (SIPOC)

1. Order Received & Flagged
+

Suppliers: Customer, Shopify/WooCommerce

Inputs: Order data, customer details.

Pain Point: No automated system to flag orders for manual vs. Printful fulfillment, relies on manual review.

2. Design Prep & Print Decision
+

Process: Verify design files, decide between in-house or outsourced printing based on complexity.

Pain Point: Decision criteria for outsourcing are not standardized, leading to variability in cost and time.

3. Item Printed & Quality Check
+

Process: Item is printed either in-house or by an external vendor, then checked for quality.

Pain Point: Delays from external printers are common; quality can be inconsistent without standardized checks.

4. Pack & Ship
+

Outputs: Shipped package, tracking info.

Customers: Wholesale clients, retail buyers.

Pain Point: Packing is manual and time-consuming; no standard packing procedure.

Project 2: Improve Online Sales Conversion

This project focuses on turning website visitors into customers by diagnosing and fixing issues in the online sales funnel, from discovery on Google to final checkout.

The Conversion Challenge

Illustrates how visitors are lost at each stage.

Root Causes & Solutions

SEO Deficiencies

Problem: Poor keyword targeting and technical SEO issues mean the right customers aren't finding the sites.

Solution: Implement on-page and technical SEO best practices, including keyword research and site speed optimization.

Lack of Trust Signals

Problem: Absence of customer reviews and security badges makes new visitors hesitate to purchase.

Solution: Implement a customer review system and display trust badges throughout the checkout process.

Poor User Experience (UX)

Problem: Complicated navigation or checkout process leads to high cart abandonment.

Solution: Streamline the checkout flow, offer guest checkout, and improve product page clarity with better descriptions and images.
